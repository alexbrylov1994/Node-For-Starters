About The Club

BTC

Connect students with industry professionals

The Business Technology Club (BTC) is a newly established student club in the University of Calgary for students interested in BTMA, coding and software engineering. We host networking events to connect students with industry professionals, and assign students to work on technology projects (like coding apps or designing small software tools) for businesses and for the university.
